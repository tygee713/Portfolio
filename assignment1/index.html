About Me

Hey there! I’m Tyler, a web developer in the tri-state area. I’ve always had a passion for computers, starting at a very young age when I was given an old Macintosh from the late 80s to play around with. This passion led me to tinker around with hardware and build my own computers, and eventually go to school for Information Technology. While going to college I worked as the webmaster for the school newspaper, and started to get interested in website development.


Now I’m a full-stack web developer in the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp, making user-friendly designs and fully-functional web applications. I also work as a sales support resource at a Cisco re-seller.


In my spare time I like to hike trails around NJ, try as many craft beers as I can, and enter video game tournaments at local arcades.
